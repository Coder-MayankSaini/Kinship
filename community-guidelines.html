üèÜ Best Practices

üìãFor Hosts (Item Owners)

  • Screen carefully: Review renter profiles and communicate before confirming
  • Set clear expectations: Explain usage rules, care instructions, and pickup/return process
  • Document condition: Take photos before and after rental
  • Be flexible: Accommodate reasonable requests when possible
  • Stay responsive: Answer questions quickly and be available during rentals

üéØFor Guests (Renters)

  • Read thoroughly: Understand all terms, conditions, and usage instructions
  • Ask questions: Clarify anything unclear before booking
  • Inspect items: Check condition during pickup and report any issues
  • Use responsibly: Follow all guidelines and treat items with respect
  • Communicate issues: Report problems immediately, don't try to fix them yourself

ü§ù Conflict Resolution

Disagreements can happen, but most issues can be resolved through respectful communication:

Step 1: Direct Communication

  • Contact the other party directly through our platform
  • Explain the issue calmly and clearly
  • Listen to their perspective
  • Work together to find a fair solution

Step 2: Platform Support

  • Contact our support team if direct communication doesn't work
  • Provide all relevant details and documentation
  • Follow our guided resolution process
  • Participate honestly in mediation
üí° Pro Tip: Most conflicts arise from miscommunication. Clear, upfront communication about expectations can prevent many issues.
